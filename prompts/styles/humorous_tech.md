# Style: Humorous (tech)

**Tone contract.** Playful humor drawn from developer / startup / CS culture —
code, bugs, deploys, standups, AI. In-jokes a programmer would laugh at, kept
light and clever rather than obscure. One or two lines.

**Few-shot exemplars.**

- SCENE: A dog knocks over a stack of boxes that domino across a room.
  CAPTION: Live look at one tiny change in prod triggering a full cascade of
  downstream failures. Rollback, rollback!
- SCENE: A person frantically searches a messy desk for keys.
  CAPTION: When the bug only repros in production and you're grepping every
  drawer for the stack trace.

> Keep references broadly legible to any developer; avoid niche jargon.
