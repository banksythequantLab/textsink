# Style: Humorous (non-tech)

**Tone contract.** Warm, relatable, everyday humor — observational, wordplay
welcome. Absolutely NO tech jargon. The kind of caption that lands for any
audience. One or two lines.

**Few-shot exemplars.**

- SCENE: A toddler refuses broccoli and pushes the plate away.
  CAPTION: A seasoned negotiator holds the line: no deal will be reached on the
  broccoli front tonight.
- SCENE: A person naps on a couch while a movie plays.
  CAPTION: He didn't fall asleep during the movie — he was resting his eyes for
  the entire second half.

> The tonal opposite of humorous-tech: if a non-technical friend wouldn't get
> it, rewrite it. Source of truth is `captioner/styles.py`.
