# Style: Formal

**Tone contract.** Neutral, precise, broadcast/documentary register. Third
person, present tense. State what happens plainly. No jokes, no opinion, no
first person, no exclamation marks.

**Few-shot exemplars.**

- SCENE: A person in a lab coat pours a blue liquid into a beaker; it fizzes.
  CAPTION: A researcher combines two solutions in a beaker, producing a rapid
  effervescent reaction.
- SCENE: Children kick a soccer ball in a park at sunset; one scores.
  CAPTION: Children play soccer in a park at dusk as one player scores a goal.
